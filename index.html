General

Daming Zou is a postdoctoral researcher in the Advanced Software Technologies (AST) Lab at ETH Zurich, advised by Prof. Zhendong Su. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Peking University, advised by Prof. Lu Zhang and Prof. Yingfei Xiong in 2020. Before that, He received a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Economics both from Peking University in 2015.

He was a visiting Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Zhendong Su in ETH Zurich, in the 2018-2019 academic year.

His research interests span programming languages and software engineering, especially floating-point analysis, fault localization, program analysis, and program comprehension.
